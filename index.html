Hello, I'm Mary Mojoyinoluwa Oladejo, a current student at the University of Waterloo, pursuing a unique degree in Global Business and Digital Arts. I aspire to be a project manager as my passion lies in planning and organizing, whether it's projects, events, or aspects of my personal life, bringing chaos to order. I love collaborating with diverse teams, cherishing the chance to learn from their perspectives and weave those insights into cohesive strategies. My journey so far has been a dynamic mix of experiences, from working as a logistics coordinator to delving into consulting, exploring digital media, and enhancing my computing skills. These experiences have ignited a passion for trying new things and discovering more about who I am. In my journey of learning, I've developed a mantra that resonates with me: "Living isn't a performance; it's a long rehearsal for the moment you realize how far you've come." This encapsulates my commitment to growth, embracing challenges, savoring the journey and being authentic. I look forward to opportunities to bring these qualities to many teams in a variety of spaces.
